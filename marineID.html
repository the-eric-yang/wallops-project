Marine Invertebrae

In this activity, we caught marine invertebraes, and identified them. Besides some macroorganisms such as the pictured stingray, there were also many microorganisms, plankton, that we were able to see with a microscope

We were able to identify the following marine invertebrates:

A blue crab caught on the boat

A spider crab caught on the boat

A clear baby squid

A turtle

A Sea Urchin

A pufferfish we caught, placed into a holding tank

A seahorse we caught, placed into a holding tank

A baby crab under a microscope

An unidentified zooplankton under the microscope

Some phytoplankton under the microscope
